Religiosity and Social Deviance Survey Analysis

Question

What is the relationship between religiosity and patterns of social deviance among undergraduate students?

Data

Designed and distributed a multifaceted survey measuring religiosity and behavioral indicators of social deviance across the Pepperdine undergraduate population. Collected and analyzed over 300 responses.

Method

Cleaned and analyzed survey responses using SPSS and Excel. Conducted correlational analysis and exploratory statistical comparisons to evaluate relationships between religiosity measures and reported deviant behaviors.

Result

Produced a final research presentation including statistical results, visualizations, and interpretations illustrating the observed relationships between religiosity and behavioral patterns within the student sample.
