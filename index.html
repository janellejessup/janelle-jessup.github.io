About

I am a computational social scientist passionate about integrating social theory with advanced quantitative and computational methodologies to analyze complex social systems. My work is grounded in rigorous statistical modeling, research design, and systematic analytical approaches, executed with methodological precision and a deeply detail-oriented approach.
